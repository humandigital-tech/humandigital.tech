HAND ID Registration

A globally unique, permanently resolvable identifier for every notable public figure — athletes, actors, creators, and the digital replicas and fictional characters they bring to life.

Why HAND Talent IDs
The proliferation problem — and its solution

Digital content is exploding across every platform. Accurately identifying and attributing human talent, virtual humans, and fictional characters has become paramount for rights management, monetization, and genuine fan engagement — and the existing infrastructure was not built for this scale.

H&D’s Citation-Backed Notability™ model — a candidate pool of 10M+ verifiable public figures drawn from independent, authoritative sources — ensures that every HAND ID represents a genuinely notable individual. Not a self-reported claim. Not a gamed metric. A verifiable public record of significance.

HAND IDs build on the DOI® Foundation registry — the same globally trusted infrastructure behind academic publishing, EIDR, and 3 billion monthly resolutions — to deliver a specialized solution for individual and character identification across the Cultural Industries.

Verifiable Credentials

Use Cases
Who needs HAND IDs — and why
Sports — College & Pro
Athlete Identity & NIL Management
500,000+ NCAA D1 athletes now have NIL rights. A portable, persistent HAND ID follows the athlete from scholarship to professional career — across transfer portal moves, school changes, endorsement deals, and broadcast platforms. For professional leagues and player associations, HAND IDs provide the neutral identifier that neither side controls but both sides can use.
Film & Television
Cast & Crew Attribution
Accurate identification of every talent across productions for residuals, royalties, and rights clearance across global streaming and broadcast platforms. HAND IDs eliminate the name disambiguation problem that causes delays in residual processing and rights clearance across partner systems.
Music
Artist & Session Musician IDs
Resolve ambiguous artist names, attribute contributions across recordings, and ensure proper compensation through interoperable identifiers that work across every DSP, label system, and royalty processing platform.
Gaming & Virtual Production
Digital Humans & Fictional Characters
Extend HAND IDs to AI-generated personas, virtual influencers, and fictional characters. Type 02 Licensed Likeness IDs create an auditable consent chain linking every synthetic likeness back to its Legal Person source — the infrastructure that AB 1836, the NO FAKES Act, and AI governance policies require.
Creator Economy
Independent Creator Attribution
Give creators a verifiable, portable identity credential that travels with their content across every platform and distribution channel. No single platform controls it. No employer owns it. It follows the creator.
Guilds & Labor Organizations
Member Identity Infrastructure
Provide guild and union members with a universally resolvable identity that supports contract enforcement, benefit tracking, and AI consent verification — governed by a neutral nonprofit, not by any studio or platform with adverse interests in labor negotiations.

Meta-Tagging &
Data Labeling

Making media IP rights-ready at scale. Not a niche technical process — a fundamental requirement for navigating the modern media landscape.

Market Context
The content explosion demands structured data

Every minute, thousands of hours of video are uploaded and millions of images are shared. Managing this volume without robust metadata is operationally impossible at scale. The global data labeling market is projected to reach tens of billions of dollars — and media is one of its fastest-growing sectors.

Three forces are converging to make meta-tagging mission-critical across both sports and entertainment: the explosion of digital content, the rise of AI and machine learning systems that depend on accurately labeled data, and increasing legal scrutiny on IP rights across global distribution.

DRIVER 01
Content Volume
Managing vast digital content without robust metadata is operationally impossible at scale.
DRIVER 02
AI & ML Dependency
Recommendation engines, rights management systems, and content analysis tools require accurately labeled data.
DRIVER 03
IP Rights Scrutiny
Complex global licensing models demand precise identification of content elements and associated rights at every level.
Rights Readiness
IP Rights Clearance — accelerated

Whether it’s a film being licensed to a streaming service, an image in an advertisement, or a music track in a video — every element must be properly attributed and cleared. Human & Digital makes that process fast, defensible, and scalable.

Granular asset identification
Precise tagging of every component within a piece of media: directors, actors, composers, locations, brands, and individual scene elements.
Streamlined rights management
Associate rich metadata with each asset to automate rights clearance: who owns which rights, what usage terms apply, when rights expire.
Enhanced syndication & monetization
Well-labeled IP is rights-ready, making it easier to package, license, and distribute to partners and platforms — opening new revenue streams.
Talent & Character Value
Human talent as a quantifiable asset

Beyond the content itself, the human talent and characters within media are increasingly recognized as quantifiable, valuable objects in the supply chain. Their presence drives audience engagement, merchandising, and brand partnerships.

Talent identification & attribution
Precise tagging of every actor, musician, voice artist, or talent featured in a piece of media, critical for royalty distribution and contractual obligations.
Character recognition & tracking
For animated features, franchises, and recurring series: identify and track specific characters across assets to enable targeted merchandising and fan engagement analytics.
