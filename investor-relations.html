Investment Thesis

Four forces converging in 2026.
One infrastructure answer.

The media and sports industries have solved content identification — EIDR, ISRC, ISBN are globally adopted standards. They have never solved talent identification at scale. Human & Digital is the commercial layer that makes the HAND universal talent identifier viable in enterprise workflows — and 2026 is the first year the market conditions simultaneously demand it.

FORCE 01 — Sports & NIL
500,000 New Rights-Holders With No Infrastructure
The NCAA NIL era created 500,000+ D1 athletes as individual rights-holders overnight. Every compliance department is drowning. Transfer portal, endorsement deals, AI likeness protections — all require verifiable, portable identity that survives school changes, conference realignments, and the turn-pro transition. Professional player associations across MLB, NFL, NBA, NHL, and international federations are simultaneously negotiating CBAs that will define AI likeness governance for the next decade. HAND is the neutral answer that no league or union controls — which is exactly why both sides can adopt it.
500K+ NCAA D1 Athletes NIL Compliance Gap MLB CBA — Dec. 2026 NFL · NBA · NHL PAs
FORCE 02 — Legislative
Active Law. Active Liability. Right Now.
CA AB 1836 and AB 2602, NY S.8420-A and S.8391, and the federal NO FAKES Act create structural economic incentive for studios, platforms, and agencies to adopt verifiable talent IDs in commercial workflows. CPRA liability exposure for SSN-based identification is a direct driver affecting every organization that processes talent data in California. This is not anticipated regulation — it is active law creating active liability today.

FORCE 03 — AI Provenance
C2PA Attests the File. HAND Attests Who Is In It.
Synthetic media, AI-generated likenesses, and digital replicas have made talent identification a trust and authenticity crisis — not just an operational inefficiency. Provenance without resolution is a label on an empty box. C2PA and CAWG membership positions H&D at the center of the content authenticity infrastructure being adopted by major platforms. HAND IDs are the talent-layer answer to the AI provenance crisis.

FORCE 04 — Labor & CBA
Both Sides of Every Negotiating Table Need This.
Guilds, unions, and labor organizations across the Cultural Industries need a talent identifier that is not controlled by studios or platforms. Studios and rights holders need a talent identifier that is not controlled by labor. HAND is governed by an independent nonprofit — structurally neutral by design. That neutrality is not a talking point. It is the architectural reason both sides of every CBA negotiation can adopt HAND without conceding anything to the other side.
